Privacy Policy for Kiku App

Last Updated: July 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Kiku ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application, Kiku.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information You Provide: When you register for an account, we collect information such as your name, username, and email address.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect the messages you send and receive within the app, including your conversations with AI profiles.
  • Technical Information: We automatically collect a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send you push notifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app's functionality.
  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Enable you to communicate with AI profiles.
  • Send you push notifications for new messages.
  • Improve our AI Models: Your conversations with our AI profiles are used to train and improve our AI's ability to provide relevant and helpful responses. Flagged messages may be reviewed by an administrator.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For generating suggested replies for our AI. We send conversation context (history, persona) to the Gemini API to get a suggestion. Google's API policy states they do not use this data to train their models.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): For sending push notifications to your device.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
